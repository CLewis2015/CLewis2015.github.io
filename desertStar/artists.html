Navajo Artists

The Navajo or Diné are one of the biggest tribes in Arizona. We carry many of their handmade products:

  • Kachinas and Shalako Kachinas
  • Dreamcatchers
  • Pottery
  • Jewelry
  • Sand Paintings

Zuni Artists

One of the Pueblo peoples of New Mexico, known for their inlay technique in jewelry using turquoise, coral, and onyx. We carry rings, pendants, earrings, and knives.

Local Artists

Gene Mazza

Gene has a unique craft. He takes pieces of broken pottery and turns them into beautiful jewelry. Bring in your own pottery pieces and take them home as earrings!

Jerry Becker

Jerry has been painting for 30 years. His watercolors feature desert plants and animals.

Brenda Schodt

Brenda creates hand-painted ornaments with colorful desert sunsets and southwest landscapes.
